Our Story

The name itself carries personal meaning. If something carries my name, it must reflect work I am proud of. That philosophy creates a consistent standard for everything I build, whether it is a cloud governance tool, a training system, or a product experiment. The brand began as a simple promise to myself: If I put my name on it, it must be worthy of the name.
